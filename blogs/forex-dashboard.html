Forex Dashboard

  • March 29, 2019
  • By Alexander Whipp

I was recently accepted into a Data Analytics in Financial Engineering, M.S. program. Those who know me, also know this program is the perfect cross between my career and my passion...

To elaborate, my passion for the past 4 years has been focused in self-teaching how to invest as a source of a livable wage, and maybe one day, substitute my full time job. This entails everything from proper money management, risk tolerance, removing emotion from the playing field, and of course knowing proper entry and exit moves.

With this in mind, I've constantly been striving to learn and develop a strategy to trade currencies for profit. Now it's time to take the next step. I put together a quick NodeJS application which polls my account every 30 seconds, and tracks my progress on my live account via Oanda APIs. I intend to add functionality down the line which includes historic account data, and more.

A few questions I received from colleagues that were interested:

  1. Why not the stock market? I've found that the equity markets don't allow for practice with a full time job.
  2. What's your goal? My goal is to acheive a 12% or greater return within a year. From there I will open a live account and try to continue that progress with an addition 3% over 3 months. This will allow me to start advertising my skills to prop firm, which will allow me to start trading te real bucks. Now, this said, I realize I am at least 2 years away from that (trial and error, and repositioning of strategy, etc).
